Privacy Policy for Savr

Last updated: January 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Savr ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By accessing or using Savr, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly and information that is automatically collected when you use the App.

A. Personal Data

When you register or update your profile, we may collect:

  • Identity Data: Name, Username, Profile Picture.
  • Contact Data: Email address, Phone number.
  • Authentication Data: Password (stored in encrypted format) or social login tokens (Google/Apple Sign-In).

B. Device & Location Data

  • Geolocation: We collect your precise location (GPS) to provide core features such as finding "Nearest Restaurants," enabling "Check-ins," and verifying your presence at a venue for gamification rewards. You can disable this in your device settings, but some features may not work.
  • Device Info: Device model, operating system, IP address, and unique device identifiers.

C. User-Generated Content (UGC)

  • Reviews & Comments: Text, ratings, and tags you submit about restaurants.
  • Photos: Images of food or venues you upload from your camera or gallery.
  • Community Activity: Questions you ask, answers you provide, and your participation in tribes.

Note: User-Generated Content is public and visible to other users of the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To create your account, display restaurants near you, and process your reviews.
  • Gamification: To calculate your Points, User Level, and Leaderboard ranking based on your activity.
  • Personalization: To recommend restaurants and Communities/Tribes based on your preferences.
  • Communication: To send you updates, security alerts, and notifications about replies to your posts (you can opt-out of marketing communications).
  • Safety: To moderate content and ban users violating our Terms of Use.

4. Permissions We Request

Depending on your device, we may request the following permissions:

  • Location (While in Use): To find nearby places and enable Check-ins.
  • Camera & Photo Library: To allow you to upload food photos and update your profile picture.
  • Notifications: To alert you about interactions (likes, replies) and app updates.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in the following situations:

  • Publicly: Your username, profile photo, level, badges, and reviews are visible to other users.
  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who perform services for us (e.g., Google Maps API for mapping, Firebase for authentication and database hosting, AWS/Cloudinary for image storage).
  • Legal Obligations: If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide the Service.

  • Right to Delete: You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • How to delete: Go to Settings > Privacy > Delete Account inside the App. or from the web app savor.bond/#/deleteaccount
  • Consequence: Your profile, points, and personal data will be permanently removed. Your public reviews may remain but will be anonymized (attributed to "Deleted User").

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
